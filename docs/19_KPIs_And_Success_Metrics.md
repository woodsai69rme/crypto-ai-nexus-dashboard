## Executive Dashboard KPIs

### Revenue Metrics

#### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
**Target**: $450K by Q4 2024
- **Free to Pro Conversion**: 5% monthly
- **Pro to Enterprise Conversion**: 2% monthly
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: $45/month
- **Revenue Growth Rate**: 15% monthly

**Calculation**:
```
MRR = (Pro Subscribers × $29) + (Enterprise Subscribers × $299)
ARPU = Total Monthly Revenue / Total Active Users
Growth Rate = (Current MRR - Previous MRR) / Previous MRR × 100
```
